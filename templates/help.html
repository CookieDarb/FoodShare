Help & Support

If you have any questions or need assistance, you're in the right place! Here are some frequently asked questions and their answers.

FAQs

1. How does FoodShare work?

FoodShare connects restaurants, donors, and NGOs to help reduce food waste and assist those in need. Users can post food donation requests or offers.

2. How can I make a food donation?

You can make a food donation by registering on our platform and posting your donation details. Our team will assist you with the process.

3. Who can use FoodShare?

FoodShare is open to restaurants, individuals, and NGOs who want to donate or receive food.

4. How can I contact support?

If you need further assistance, you can reach us at support@foodshare.com.
